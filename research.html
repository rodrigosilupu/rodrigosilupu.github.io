Competition, Liberalization and Productivity: The Case of Peru 2003-2017

                    Mario D. Tello Pacheco & Cristina J. Tello-Trillo
                    This research was a consulting project required by the World Bank. We analyzed 
                    the impact of competition on the growth of total productivity of manufacturing companies, 
                    using estimates of the effects of Peru's three preferential trade agreements with the 
                    US, China, and the EU for the period 2003-2017. 
                    In this research, I was in charge of the literature review, the construction of 
                    the database, regressions, and final output tables.

Cadenas Globales de Valor y Productos de Exportación de los Países de la Comunidad Andina 2000-2019

                    Mario D. Tello Pacheco
                It was a consulting project required by the Andean Community of Nations (in Spanish CAN). 
                The main objective of this research was to give policy recommendations to boost trade 
                through Global Value Chains (GVC) in the CAN countries. In this sense, eight indicators 
                related to the GVC were calculated, in addition to providing information on all the 
                export markets of the CAN at different levels: level of 3 sectors (primary,
                manufacturing, and services), in 24 industries, at the level of tariff headings
                (2, 6 and 10 digits, the latter being those that represent a product) and at the business
                level. 
                In this research, I was in charge of the construction of the database, the elaboration 
                of the GVC indicators, tables related to the export and import products of the CAN 
                countries, and the regressions and different statistical tables.
